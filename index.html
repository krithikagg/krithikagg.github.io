MakerGaon Fellowship by MakerGhat

Become a changemaker in the rural parts of India

What is the MakerGaon Fellowship? Lead from the ground – transform rural learning through makerspaces, hands-on innovation, and local leadership. Fellows will: • Create change that matters most – Grow resilience, adaptability, and an entrepreneurial mindset. • Bring learning to life – Lead hands-on problem-solving, sparking confidence, creativity, and collaboration. • Build what lasts – Develop local talent, partnerships, and a lasting culture of making.
